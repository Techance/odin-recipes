ADOBO

A delicious Adobo

A delicious and famous filipino delicacy

INGREDIENTS:

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Bayleaf

STEPS:

  1. Heat Vegetable oil and cook chicken
  2. Add Onion and Garlic
  3. Pour in Soy Sauce, Vinegar, Black Pepper and Bayleaf
  4. Simmer until the chicken is tender
